The Problem

AI assistants weren't built for real work.

They forget everything

Start a new chat, start from scratch. ChatGPT doesn't know what you were working on yesterday.

Your stuff gets mixed together

Work projects bleed into personal. Client A's info shows up when you're helping Client B.

You have zero control

They store your conversations, train on your data, and decide what you can access. You're renting someone else's brain — on their terms.

The Solution

Ironbeard is different.

Workspaces that actually separate

Each workspace has its own AI personality, its own memory, its own tools — including MCP servers. They don't touch each other.

A vault that remembers

Drop files into your vault. The AI indexes them and can reference them anytime. Your research stays with you.

Your keys. Your machine. Your cost.

Bring your own Claude API key — no middleman subscription. Your files and memory stay local. A $450 laptop runs it fine.

Smart about cost

Claude handles the thinking. Gemini and Haiku handle the grunt work. You get top-tier AI without top-tier bills.
