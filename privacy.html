Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ironbeard ("the Software"), developed by Ironbeard ("we," "us," or "our"), handles your information. We are committed to being transparent about our data practices — which, in short, means we don't collect your data.

1. Our Core Privacy Principle

Ironbeard does not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your personal data. The Software runs entirely on your local machine. We have no servers that receive your information, no analytics dashboards, and no telemetry. We cannot see what you do with the Software.

2. Information We Collect

None. We do not collect any information from you through the Software. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, address)
  • No usage data or analytics
  • No telemetry or crash reports
  • No device identifiers or fingerprinting
  • No cookies, tracking pixels, or similar technologies
  • No browsing or activity history

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The Software stores certain data locally on your computer to function. This data never leaves your machine through us:

Data Type Storage Location Purpose
API Keys Operating System Keyring Authentication with third-party AI services
User Content Local Application Data Your inputs, outputs, and saved work
Application Settings Local Configuration Files Your preferences and configuration

You have full control over this data. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the Software and removing its associated data files from your computer.

4. Third-Party Services

Important: While we do not collect your data, the Software connects to third-party AI services using API keys that you provide. When you use these services, your data is transmitted directly from your computer to those providers.

The Software may connect to the following third-party services (depending on your configuration):

  • Anthropic — When you use AI features powered by Claude. Your prompts and content are sent to Anthropic's servers and are subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Google — When you use AI features powered by Google's services. Your data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We strongly encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you connect to through the Software. We have no control over how these providers collect, use, or store your data.

5. Sensitive Information

You may choose to input personal, sensitive, or confidential information into the Software. Please be aware that:

  • Any data you enter may be sent to third-party API providers as part of normal Software functionality.
  • You are solely responsible for deciding what information to input.
  • We recommend exercising caution with highly sensitive data such as financial records, medical information, passwords, or other confidential material.

6. Data Security

Your API keys are stored in your operating system's secure keyring, which provides encryption at the OS level. However, the developer is not a professional security engineer, and no guarantees are made regarding the security of the Software. You use the Software at your own risk. Please refer to our Terms and Conditions for the full disclaimer of warranties.

7. Children's Privacy

The Software is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children, as we do not collect information from anyone.

8. LemonSqueezy (Payment Processing)

If you purchase a license for the Software, your transaction is processed by LemonSqueezy, our payment provider and Merchant of Record. LemonSqueezy may collect personal and payment information necessary to process your purchase. This data is handled according to LemonSqueezy's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your full payment details.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any of your personal data, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete. All of your data resides on your own computer under your full control. If you wish to remove all data associated with the Software, simply uninstall it and delete its local data files.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Software after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ironbeardai@gmail.com.
